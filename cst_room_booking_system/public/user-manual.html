Introduction

The CST Room Booking System is an online hostel room allocation platform for students of the College of Science and Technology (CST), Royal University of Bhutan. It replaces the manual room-assignment process with a fast, transparent, and fair digital system.

Who uses this system?

RoleWhoWhat they can do
StudentAll enrolled studentsActivate account, browse hostels, book / unbook a room bed
CounselorAssigned hostel counselorsMonitor occupancy and bookings for their assigned hostel
AdminHostel office staffManage students, rooms, hostels, counselors, booking periods
Access URL Open your browser and go to the system URL provided by your college IT office.

Getting Started — Account Activation Student

Every new student must activate their account before they can log in. Activation is a one-time process that verifies your college email address.

Step-by-step activation

  1. On the login page, click "Activate Account" button (below the Login button).

  2. Enter your Student Number (e.g. 02230137) and click Send OTP.

  3. Check your college email inbox (XXXXXXXX.cst@rub.edu.bt) for a 6-digit One-Time Password (OTP).

  4. Enter the OTP on the verification screen and click Verify.

  5. Create a strong password (minimum 6 characters) and click Set Password.

  6. You will be redirected to the login page with a success message. You can now log in.

OTP Expiry The OTP is valid for only 10 minutes. If it expires, restart the activation process.
Tip Check your spam/junk folder if the OTP email does not arrive within 2 minutes.
Rate limit You can request a maximum of 3 OTPs within any 10-minute window.

How to book a room

  1. From the dashboard, click View Rooms or navigate to a hostel.

  2. Select a floor from the sidebar.

  3. The floor plan shows all rooms. Click on any green (available) room tile.

  4. A confirmation dialog appears — read the room number, then click Confirm.

  5. Wait for the booking to process. A success message appears and a confirmation email is sent to your college email.

Booking restrictions
  • You can only book one room at a time.
  • The room must match your academic year (e.g., Year 1 students can only book Year 1 rooms).
  • The room must match your gender (male/female hostels are separate).
  • Bookings are only possible during an active booking period set by the admin.
Tip Check your email after booking — a confirmation with your room details will be sent automatically.

How to unbook a room

  1. Navigate to your booked room on the floor plan (shown in blue).

  2. Click on it. A confirmation dialog will ask if you want to unbook.

  3. Click Confirm to release your booking.

Unbooking is only allowed during an active booking period. If the period is closed, contact your hostel admin.

Admin Dashboard Admin

Administrators have full control over the system. After login, admins land on /admin_dashboard.

Dashboard overview

The main dashboard shows recent bookings, system-wide stats (total rooms, occupied, available), and quick-access navigation to all management sections.

6.1

Student Management Admin

Navigate to Admin Dashboard → Students.

Viewing students

  • All students are listed in a paginated table
  • Search by name, student number, department, or year using the search bar

Adding a single student

  1. Click Add Student.

  2. Fill in all required fields: name, student number, email, gender, year, department, phone number.

  3. Click Save.

Bulk import (CSV / JSON)

  1. Click Import.

  2. Upload a .csv or .json file containing student records.

  3. The system validates and imports all valid records.

Editing a student

Click the Edit icon on any student row, update the fields, and click Save.

Deleting a student

Click the Delete icon. Confirm the dialog. All associated bookings are also removed.

Caution Deleting a student permanently removes their account and booking history.
6.2

Room Management Admin

Navigate to Admin Dashboard → Rooms.

Viewing rooms

Rooms are listed by hostel and floor. Each row shows room number, capacity, current occupancy, status, and year restriction.

Editing room settings

  1. Click Edit on a room row.

  2. Change capacity, year restriction, or gender restriction as needed.

  3. Click Save.

Disabling / enabling a room

Click the Disable toggle to put a room in maintenance mode (students cannot book it). Click again to re-enable.

Manually allocating students

  1. Click Allocate on a room.

  2. Search for and select students from the list.

  3. Click Confirm Allocation.

Deallocating students

Click Deallocate on a room, select the students to remove, and confirm.

Room status values

6.3

Hostel Management Admin

Navigate to Admin Dashboard → Hostel.

Viewing hostels

All 10 hostels are listed with current capacity, occupancy rate, gender designation, and status.

Editing a hostel

  1. Click Edit on a hostel.

  2. Update gender designation (male/female) or status (active/inactive).

  3. Click Save.

Floor year allocations

You can restrict specific floors to specific academic years (e.g., Floor 1 → Year 1 only).

  1. Open a hostel and scroll to Floor Allocations.

  2. Select the year for each floor.

  3. Click Save Allocations.

Special rule Year 4 allocated floors also allow Year 5 and Year 6 students to book.
6.4

Counselor Assignment Admin

Counselors must be existing users (students) who are promoted to the counselor role and assigned to a hostel.

Assigning a counselor

  1. Navigate to Admin Dashboard → Counselor section.

  2. Search for the student by name or student number.

  3. Select an available hostel from the dropdown.

  4. Click Assign.

Removing a counselor

Find the counselor in the list and click Remove. The user reverts to the regular student role.

Frequently Asked Questions

I did not receive the OTP email. What should I do?
Check your spam/junk folder first. If it's not there, wait 2 minutes and try requesting a new OTP. Remember you can only request 3 OTPs per 10-minute window. If the problem persists, contact IT support.
I get "Account not activated" when I try to log in.
You need to complete the one-time activation process. Click "Activate Account" on the login page and follow the steps in Section 1.
I clicked a room but got "Access Denied" for year restriction.
That floor or room is reserved for a different academic year. Check other floors or hostels for rooms allocated to your year. Contact admin if you believe this is an error.
I clicked a room but got "Access Denied" for gender restriction.
The hostel is designated for the opposite gender. Please choose a hostel that matches your gender.
The "Unbook" option is not available on my room.
Unbooking is only permitted during an active booking period. The admin controls when this window is open. Contact the hostel office for assistance outside this period.
I booked a room but did not receive a confirmation email.
Check your spam folder. The email is sent to your college email (XXXXXXXX.cst@rub.edu.bt). If you still don't see it, your booking is still valid — you can check your booking on the floor plan (your room appears highlighted).
I forgot my password.
Password reset is handled by the hostel admin. Contact the hostel office with your student ID to get a password reset.
Can I book more than one room?
No. Each student is allowed a maximum of one active room booking at a time.
The room appears available but I cannot click it.
The booking period may be closed, or the room may have a year/gender restriction. Check the room colour legend and ensure you are logged in.
